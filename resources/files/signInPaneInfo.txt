How to sign in:
 - scan your fob while the text box is focused
 - type your name/Case ID into the text box and hit "Sign In"/Enter
 - Click "View Unaccounted-for Members", find yout name, and click it
   - You can then sign in normally or as an excused absence
   
**IMPORTANT: DO NOT HAVE THE ATTENDANCE FILE OPEN IN EXCEL WHEN YOU CLICK "Save" OR "Save and Return to Setup"! THE SAVE WILL FAIL, THE FILE MAY BECOME CORRUPTED, AND YOU ARE LIKELY TO LOSE DATA **

If you would like to save the current data, click the "Save" button.
If you are done for the night (i.e. everyone has signed in, or you want to mark everyone who hasn't signed in as being absent), click "Save and Exit".
